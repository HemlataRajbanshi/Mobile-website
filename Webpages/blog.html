Impact of Technology

Technology is scientific knowledge which is designed to make human life easier, but sometimes it create new problems. As it brings many changes in our life, different people use it differently which makes positive and negative impact on people.

Positive impact of Technology

Technology has make human life easier. With the help of mobile technology we can talk to our friends, family and also with strangers living far away from us. With the help of technology we are able to learn new things, students are getting better knowledge and helps them to get high marks. Learning process is easier and entertaining. The consumption of time is less. Due to technology, today we are able to travel distant places within hours, which took many days or years to reach in old days. Technology is useful in every field such as health sector, schools, offices, etc.

Negative impact of technology

Technology like smartphones, games, laptops, etc distract people from achieving a state of flow of work. There is lack of real connection between people. Instead of taking care personally they are sending sms or calling on important occasions. Childrens/ teenagers are specially getting addicted with phones, different webseries and online games which are making them physically weak.Due to technology sone couples are geting divorced, relation with in family is getting weak. Heavy usage of fertilizers soil is losion its natural fertility and varities of plants are extinct.
